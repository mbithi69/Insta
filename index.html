Our Vision

To be the most efficient microfinance institution in Kenya by being cashless, paperless and data-driven to offer the best value, most flexible and most customer-oriented financial services in the market.

Our Mission

To grow, build and maximize the potential of the businesses of the low income and unbanked of Kenya through the provision of affordable, flexible and customer-oriented financial services.

Core Values

Innovation

Develop new ideas to enable us to be the most efficient microfinance institution in Kenya, delivering innovative and competitive financial services by using the latest technology and data analytics.

Integrity

Do what is right,treat clients,partners and each other with utmost respect,honesty and uphold the highest ethical standards.

Customer Focus

Delivering process oriented and quality driven services aligned with our customer needs.

Excellence

Strive to deliver first class service through best talent and continous personal and professional development aiming to create value to our staff,clients and shareholders.
